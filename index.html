About GDSC

It's a program for university students to learn mobile and web development skills. It is a program that includes workshops, mentorship, and networking opportunities.

Google Developer Student Clubs are university based community groups for students interested in Google developer technologies.

Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome.

By joining a GDSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.

Events

BOOTCAMPS

Event to help students learn and improve their skills in mobile and web development. These bootcamps usually consist of intensive, hands-on training sessions focused on specific topics or technologies, such as machine learning, cloud computing, or web development frameworks.

HACKATONS

Event where individuals or teams come together to develop a project or solution to a specific problem or challenge within a limited time period. Hackathons are a great opportunity for students to showcase their skills and creativity while learning from each other and industry experts. Google Developer Student Clubs often collaborate with other organizations to host hackathons, and they offer mentorship and resources to help participants succeed. Hackathons can also be a great way for students to network with other developers and industry professionals.
